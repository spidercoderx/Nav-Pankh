About Our Oil

Premium Quality From Farm to Table

Nav Pankh peanut oil is extracted from the finest quality peanuts through a careful cold-pressing process that preserves all the natural nutrients and flavor. Our oil is then refined using modern, chemical-free methods to ensure purity and longevity.

We work directly with local farmers who grow peanuts without harmful pesticides, ensuring you get the healthiest oil possible. Each batch is tested for quality and purity before packaging.
